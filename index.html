Marking your experiences...

The Institute for Innovations in Medical Education (IIME) has collaborated with Dr. Linda Tewksbury and Ms. Lakesha Baker to create meaningful Patient Log visualizations for your Pediatrics Clerkship clinical encounters.

As a member of the October Pediatrics Clerkship rotation, you will be the ﬁrst among your peers to be able to review your patient log domain progress at-a-glance. This visualization system is speciﬁcally designed to provide you with important information to help guide you through your clerkship experience. IIME incorporated feedback from both our student community and our NYU School of Medicine leadership to ensure that this system meaningfully relates overall clerkship objectives to your individual learning pathways.

The ultimate goal of this pilot is to develop a personalized digital progress console, which will extend beyond the patient log application. Our aim is to provide a holistic system that features curricular transparency, enhances engagement within our community, and highlights opportunities to tailor your development. IIME looks forward to your feedback, as your recommendations shape our goals and help reﬁne our mission to serve you through technological innovations within the NYU School of Medicine learning ecosystem.

How it works

This new system is currently linked to Patient Log.

The ﬁrst time you check a highlighted domain in the Pediatrics Clerkship patient log form, the system will send you an email with a customized message from your program director.

This email provides important information about why this domain is relevant to your current responsibilities as a medical student, as well as your future role as a clinician.

Next

About the emails

The system will send you a maximum of 15 emails. 1 email for each of the 14 highlighted domains and 1 ﬁnal email recognizing the submissions for the 14 domains.

The highlighted domains are:
• Growth
• Nutrition
• Development
• Behavior
• Upper Respiratory Tract
• Lower Respiratory Tract
• Health Maintenance
• Gastrointestinal Tract
• Dermatologic System
• Central Nervous System
• Emergent Clinical Problem
• Chronic Medical Problem
• Unique Condition: Fever without source
• Unique Conditions common to the young infant

This new system is designed for your personal use.
Administration will not have access to your personal progress view.

You ATM Design

It's you at-the-moment! You will be able to access your progress view at all times, whether or not you have submitted a log with a highlighted domain.

Timing is everything

Once you submit a log for one of the 14 domains, you should receive an email within 12 hours. Your progress view should also reflect your domain updates.

Master your domains

Volume Control

Only the ﬁrst log submission for the 14 highlighted domains will generate an email and update your progress view.
